About

Designer, artist, technologist.

One practice, fifteen years, a single throughline: tools and systems for coordination, trust, and care.

I'm Saraswathi — a product design lead who takes products from zero to shipped, and an artist whose installations and experiments keep asking the same questions the products do. The work moves through three arcs: making complex systems legible (Bloomberg, Chase, MetaMask), making value and trust designable (Circles, Ethereal, One Token), and building technology in service of care and repair (Hypha, Lobby4Me, repair, and the restorative-computing framework I've been developing since 2022).

Today I run a small studio practice, ship AI-native products for clients and for myself, and consult on growth and 0→1 strategy. I hold a Master's from NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program and a B.A. in Technocultural Studies from UC Davis, with certifications spanning MIT (Building AI Products, 2024) to NN/g UX. I've spoken on civic tech, alternative economics, and regenerative design from Climate Week NYC to the Basic Income Earth Network.

Now

I'm open to founding product designer, sr. product design lead, and head of design roles, specializing in 0→1 and AI-native products. Los Angeles, remote, or open to relocation for the right role.
